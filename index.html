Why This Workshop Exists

Most veterans are making TSP decisions without the full picture.

The Thrift Savings Plan is one of the most powerful retirement tools available to service members — but only if you know how to use it. After 21 years of service and hundreds of financial planning conversations with veterans, I've seen the same costly gaps come up again and again.

TSP fund allocations that haven't been reviewed in years — still set to defaults from enlistment day.

Rolling TSP to an IRA without understanding the tax tradeoffs — and locking in a costly mistake.

Missing the contribution window during deployment or high-income years — money left on the table permanently.

Not understanding how TSP interacts with your pension, Social Security, and VA disability compensation.

Outdated beneficiary designations that don't reflect your current family situation.

Facing separation with no one to help model the financial decision — and making it under pressure.

These aren't rare edge cases. They're the default for veterans who haven't had access to a CFP® who actually understands military compensation. This workshop fixes that.
