Why This Workshop Exists

Most veterans are leaving serious money on the table with their TSP — and don't know it.

The Thrift Savings Plan is one of the most powerful retirement tools in the world. But it's also one of the most misunderstood. After 21 years of service and hundreds of financial planning conversations with veterans, I've seen the same mistakes made over and over again.

Leaving money in the wrong TSP fund for decades — and watching the market work against you instead of for you.

Not knowing when — and whether — to roll your TSP to an IRA. Getting this wrong has permanent tax consequences.

Missing the window to make TSP contributions count during deployment or high-income years.

Not understanding how your TSP interacts with your pension, Social Security, and VA disability compensation.

Choosing the wrong beneficiary designation — a mistake that could cost your spouse tens of thousands of dollars.

Transitioning out and making a TSP decision under time pressure without fully understanding the tradeoffs.

None of this is your fault. The TSP was designed by the government — not for simplicity. This workshop exists to change that.
